Steven Francisco

I recently finished a Web Development course at Epicodus in Portland, OR. While at Epicodus I spent forty plus hours a week for four months learning how to write code first-hand(the entire course is hands on coding, you build apps all day at Epicodus) from scratch. The course that I chose to take focused on Ruby, Javascript, and Ruby on Rails.

I also just finished an internship at Spotcolor Marketing. While at Spotcolor I learned how to build and maintain websites using Wordpress. My main project was to build a website for a non-profit that works with troubled youth in Oregon City. That was a rewarding first “real world” project for me to do as I felt it was for a very good cause. I’m quite proud of how it turned out too(see here). I also helped out with other website issues coming in from clients. It was a great experience and gave me first-hand knowledge and experiencing in meeting the customers needs.

What I love about programming is its abstract nature and all of the epiphanies that occur throughout the learning process.

As an artist, I'm interested in the creative aspects of programming and hope to explore new avenues of creativity in this field.
